Straw-hat Pirates

Monkey D. Luffy

Current Bounty: 3,000,000,000

Luffy is the captain of the Straw Hat Pirates, and dreamt of being a pirate since childhood from the influence of his idol and mentor Red-Haired Shanks. At the age of 17, Luffy sets sail from the East Blue Sea to the Grand Line in search of the legendary treasure, One Piece, to succeed Gol D.Roger

Roronoa Zoro

Current Bounty: 1,111,000,000

Zoro was born 21 years ago in the East Blue to Tera and Roronoa Arashi, a descendant of the Shimotsuki Family. By the time Zoro was 8 years old, his father had already been killed in a battle against pirates threatening Shimotsuki Village.

Jinbe

Current Bounty: 1,100,000,000

Jinbe is a fish-man and a key member of the Straw Hat Pirates in the "One Piece" series. He's known for his fish-man karate skills, water manipulation, and unwavering loyalty to his crewmates. As the crew's helmsman, he guides their ship and offers sage advice. Jinbe is a symbol of justice and equality in the "One Piece" world.

Sanji

Current Bounty: 1,032,000,000

Sanji is a member of the Straw Hat Pirates in "One Piece." He's the crew's chef and a formidable fighter, using powerful leg-based techniques. His dream is to find the All Blue, a legendary sea with diverse fish. Sanji is known for his chivalry, love for women, and rivalry with Zoro.

Nico Robin

Current Bounty: 930,000,000

Nico Robin is the archaeologist in "One Piece." Her Devil Fruit power allows her to sprout extra limbs. She's driven by a desire to uncover the world's true history and is a knowledgeable and reserved member of the Straw Hat Pirates.

God Ussop

Current Bounty: 500,000,000

Usopp is a Straw Hat Pirates member in "One Piece," a gifted sharpshooter with a knack for storytelling. He aspires to become a brave warrior of the sea, like his father Yasopp of the Red-Haired Pirates. Despite his tall tales, Usopp shows bravery and resourcefulness when needed.

Franky

Current Bounty: 394,000,000

Franky is a Straw Hat Pirates member in "One Piece." He's the crew's shipwright and the builder of their ship, the Thousand Sunny. Known for his mechanical skills and his dream to create the ultimate ship, "Pluton." Franky's flamboyant personality and cyborg modifications make him unique.

Brook

Current bounty: 383,000,000

Brook is a Straw Hat Pirates member in "One Piece." He's a living skeleton and the crew's musician. Brook's dream is to reunite with his former crew and uncover the world's true history. Known for his quirky personality, he's an invaluable member of the crew.

Nami

Current Bounty: 366,000,000

Nami, from "One Piece," is the Straw Hat Pirates' navigator. She guides the ship and aspires to create accurate maps of the world. Known for her sharp intellect and kind heart, Nami plays a crucial role in the crew's adventures.

Tony Tony Chopper

Current Bounty: 1,000

Chopper, a member of "One Piece's" Straw Hat Pirates, is a reindeer with a human form due to the Human-Human Fruit. He serves as the ship's doctor and dreams of becoming a great healer. Known for his cuteness and innocence, Chopper is a valuable and lovable crew member.
